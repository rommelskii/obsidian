- There are 3 requirements
	- Console port on the SWITCH
	- Serial port on the PC
	- Serial to Console cable
- Sometimes USB is used as a cable
- DB-9 connection
	- Used on older serial ports (RJ45->DB-9)
- 8N1 connection
	- 9600 baud rate
	- No hfc 
	- 8-bit ASCII
	- No parity bits 
	- one stop bit
